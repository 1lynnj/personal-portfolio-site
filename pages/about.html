About Me

Hello! I'm a 20-year veterinary professional pivoting to a career in software development. I'm currently a student at Ada Developers Academy learning full stack web development including Python, Flask, RESTful API, SQL, SQLAlchemy, HTML, CSS, Javascript and React.

Working in a high-volume specialty veterinary emergency and surgery practice, I learned the value of working cooperatively within a team and the necessity for creative problem solving when the cases didn't follow the rules. I discovered my love of tech when researching and implementing solutions to reduce face-to-face interactions in a healthcare field during the pandemic.

Check out my portfolio to see what I've been up to. Reach out if you have questions or just want to talk coding.
